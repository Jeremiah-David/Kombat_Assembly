Welcome to Kombat Assembly!

                
In Kombat Assembly, you and another player pick an attack from the list above your character. When your both ready press the
                    "Fight!" button and see who wins! The losing player will lose health, represented by the heart above your character. If your health reaches zero, 
                    lose and your opponent wins! If you drain your opponent's health, you win! 

                
    
                    
  • Attack 1 beats Attack 2 & 3
    • 
                    
  • Attack 2 beats Attack 3 & 4
    • 
                    
  • Attack 3 beats Attack 4 & 5
    • 
                    
  • Attack 4 beats Attack 5 & 1
    • 
                    
  • Attack 5 beats Attack 1 & 2
