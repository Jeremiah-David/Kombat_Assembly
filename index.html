Welcome to Kombat Assembly!

At the Temple, the Temple Cat has gotten tired of being the pet, and decided she wants to run the place! The CareTaker is trying to regain order, and they have decided on a Fighting Contest to see gets control of the Temple!

In Kombat Assembly, you take control of the Cat and battle for the right to run the temple. If there is a second player they take control of the Temple CareTaker. First pick if your fighting against the computer or another player. Pick your attack from the buttons over your character. If in Two Player Mode, wait for Player 2 to pick an attack also. Then click the "Fight!" button and see who wins! The losing player will lose health, represented by the heart above your character. If your health reaches zero, you lose and your opponent wins! If you drain your opponent's health, you win!

  • Attack 1 beats Attack 2 & 3
  • Attack 2 beats Attack 3 & 4
  • Attack 3 beats Attack 4 & 5
  • Attack 4 beats Attack 5 & 1
  • Attack 5 beats Attack 1 & 2
  • If you hit your opponent 3 times without getting hit you can pull of your SUPER SPECIAL FINISHING FATATLITY ULTRA FINISHER!!!
